# Research v1 — Initial B2B Opportunity Scan (Feb 2026)

## What we did
1. Scraped 113K app reviews from App Store & Google Play across 15+ B2B categories
2. Analyzed pain points, pricing frustrations, and feature gaps
3. Built financial models for top opportunities
4. Scored each on solo-founder fit (market size, competition, margins, complexity, self-serve)

## Key findings
- **Horizontal SaaS is dead for solo founders** — too many competitors, too much capital needed
- **Vertical/niche is the play** — specific industry + specific pain = defensible
- **Top 5 opportunities** (by score):
  1. Simple Helpdesk (30/35) → became GlowDesk
  2. Field Service Management (28/35)
  3. Simple CRM (27/35)
  4. Restaurant Management (26/35)
  5. Construction PM (25/35)

## Limitations (why we're doing v2)
- Data was mostly app store reviews — biased toward mobile users, not decision-makers
- Reddit data was scraped via Brave Search snippets — incomplete
- No structured review data from G2/Capterra (where B2B buyers actually review)
- Limited to English — no Spanish/LATAM data
- No USA→Colombia arbitrage analysis
- Customer discovery was insufficient (2 comments, 5 DMs, 1 reply)
