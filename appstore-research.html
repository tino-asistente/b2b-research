#1 Simple Receipt/Expense Scanner for Small Business 31/35 Overkill complexity ▼
Apps analyzed: Expensify, QuickBooks
ICP: Solo freelancers, contractors, small business owners (1-5 people) doing their own bookkeeping
WTP: $5-15/mo
Current workaround: Spreadsheets, photo folders, shoebox of receipts

💬 What Users Ask For

A dead-simple app to snap receipts, categorize expenses, and export for tax time. No team admin, no chat, no social features.

🚫 Why The App Won't Fix It

Expensify pivoted to a 'super app' (chat, travel, etc). QuickBooks bundles expenses into full accounting. Neither will simplify — their revenue depends on upselling.

🛠️ How to Build Cheaper/Simpler

Mobile-first app: camera → OCR (Google Vision API) → auto-categorize → export CSV/PDF. No team features, no chat. Just receipts.

⚠️ Risks

Crowded space, but incumbents are all overbuilt. Wave, Dext exist but also complex.

⏰ Why Now

Expensify's 2024-2025 'New Expensify' rewrite has infuriated users. QuickBooks prices keep rising. Window of angry users looking for alternatives.

📈 Path to $10K MRR

Launch on Product Hunt + Reddit r/smallbusiness. $10/mo × 1000 users. App store distribution. Content marketing on 'Expensify alternatives'.

📝 Methodology

Data Collection (Feb 25, 2026):

  • Scraped 20 B2B SaaS apps using google-play-scraper and app-store-scraper npm packages
  • Google Play: up to 150 most recent reviews per app, sorted by newest
  • App Store: up to 50 most recent reviews per app (US store), sorted by recent
  • Filtered for 1-2 star reviews only
  • Note: App Store scraper returned 0 results for some apps (Jira, Monday, Zendesk, Freshdesk, Loom, Zapier, PagerDuty, Intercom) — likely API/regional limitations
  • Zapier's Google Play app returned 0 reviews (app may be too new or delisted)

Categorization: Each negative review was auto-categorized via keyword matching into: PRICING, MISSING_FEATURE, COMPLEXITY, BUGS, SUPPORT. Reviews can match multiple categories. Default category is BUGS if no keywords match.

Scoring: Each opportunity scored 1-5 on 7 dimensions (35 max) with a solo-founder lens: penalizing network effects, enterprise sales, and regulatory complexity; rewarding self-serve distribution, simple MVPs, and quick time-to-revenue.

Limitations: Keyword-based categorization is imperfect. Review samples are recent but not exhaustive. App Store data was incomplete for several apps. Quotes are real but represent a snapshot in time.
